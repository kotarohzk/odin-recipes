Bibimbap

Description

Bibimbap simply translates to “mixed rice with meat and assorted vegetables“. You can make endless variations to this dish depending on your preference and dietary requirements (e.g. microgreens bibimbap) and also based on the dish/bowl it is served in (e.g. dolsot bibimbap and yangpun bibimbap).

Ingredients

Meat and meat sauce

  • 100g beef mince
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp minced garlice

Vegetables and others

  • 250g spinach
  • 350g bean sprouts
  • 100g shiitake mushroom
  • 120g carrots
  • 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 3 cups of steamed rice
  • 3 eggs
  • cooking oil

Bibimbap sauce

  • 2 Tbsp gochujang
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1 Tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp minced garlic

Steps

  1. Prepare and cook all meat and vegetable ingredients separately
  2. Mix the bibimbap sauce in a bowl
  3. Put the rice into a bowl and add the meat, assorted vegetables, seasoned seaweed, bibimbap sauce, and egg on top of the rice. Serve.
